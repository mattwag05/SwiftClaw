Phase 4: Streaming + Polish — What should the typing experience feel like?

The assistant's response will arrive token-by-token. How should it render?

A

Instant append (Recommended)

Each token appears immediately as it arrives — like ChatGPT/Claude.ai. The text grows character-by-character with no animation. A blinking cursor (▌) at the end indicates generation is in progress. Simple, fast, familiar.

Typewriter effect

Tokens arrive and render with a subtle fade-in animation. Smoother visually but adds complexity and slight latency. Can feel sluggish on fast backends.
